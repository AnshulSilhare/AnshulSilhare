### 🤖 ML & Predictive Intelligence

#### 📦 [DataCo Supply Chain: AI Delivery Risk Predictor](https://github.com/AnshulSilhare/ai-dataco-supply-chain) | [🚀 Live Demo](https://anshulsilhare-ai-dataco-supply-chain.hf.space)
*End-to-end Machine Learning web application to predict global shipping delays before they occur.*
- **The Challenge:** Delays in global supply chains lead to stockouts and increased inventory holding costs.
- **The Methodology:** Engineered a Random Forest classifier on 180,000+ records using Python and Scikit-Learn; processed 240+ features via One-Hot Encoding and StandardScaler, deployed via Streamlit & Gradio on Hugging Face Spaces.
- **The Outcome:** Delivered a production-ready dashboard providing real-time, probability-based delay risk percentages for both individual and bulk order uploads.
- **Metrics:** **180K+** records trained on | **80%** precision score | **240+** engineered features.

### 🚚 Operations & Supply Chain Analytics

#### 🗺️ [Logistics Network Design: 2-Day Delivery Optimization](https://github.com/AnshulSilhare/Logistics-Network-Optimization) | [🗺️ Interactive Map](https://AnshulSilhare.github.io/Logistics-Network-Optimization/map.html)
*Strategic network analysis to achieve 90% US coverage within 48 hours.*
- **The Challenge:** Designing an optimal distribution network to balance freight costs vs. speed.
- **The Methodology:** Conducted Geodesic Transit Time Analysis and economic modeling to design a "Lean 5-Node" distribution network (Redlands, Robbinsville, Louisville, Coppell, Lakeland).
- **The Outcome:** Validated 94% customer population coverage within 2 days while reducing fuel consumption.
- **Metrics:** **5-Node** Hub-and-Spoke model | **94%** Population Coverage | **-18%** Fuel Consumption.

#### 📈 [Pharma Demand Forecasting: Statistical Model Selection](https://github.com/AnshulSilhare/Pharma-Demand-Forecasting)
*Matching the math to market behavior for a multi-product portfolio.*
- **The Challenge:** High volatility in some SKUs (Product A) vs. stable demand in others (Product B) skewing inventory levels.
- **The Methodology:** Segmented product portfolio by coefficient of demand variation. Applied **Exponential Smoothing (Alpha = 0.5)** to capture volatile demand surges and **Moving Averages** for stable lines.
- **The Outcome:** Reduced procurement lag, minimized expiry waste, and optimized safety stock levels to prevent stockouts.
- **Metrics:** dual-engine forecasting strategy | **SKU Profiling** | waste & stockout mitigation.

#### 🏭 [Warehouse Layout Optimization: Flow Engineering](https://github.com/AnshulSilhare/Warehouse-Layout-Optimization)
*Redesigning facility layout to resolve picking bottlenecks.*
- **The Challenge:** Excessive picker travel time and congestion at inbound/outbound zones.
- **The Methodology:** Redesigned a traditional warehouse floor plan using Lean principles. Implemented "Cross-Dock" style conveyor systems, segregated Inbound/Outbound zones, and mapped high-turnover SKUs closer to packing lanes (ABC Analysis).
- **The Outcome:** Theoretically increased throughput capacity by **20%** by eliminating layout overlaps and minimizing picker travel distance.

#### 🏥 [Medical Device Value Stream Mapping](https://github.com/AnshulSilhare/Value-Stream-Mapping)
*Visualizing the gap between planning and reality in medical manufacturing.*
- **The Challenge:** Significant delays in the sterilization pipeline skewing central planning data.
- **The Methodology:** Mapped the "Current State" flow in Lucidchart, exposing a **96+ hour delay** between assembly and sterilization.
- **The Outcome:** Identified "Trunk Stock" (hidden inventory carried by sales reps) as the primary cause of data skewness.

### 💼 Databases & Business Research

#### 🗄️ [SQL Data Warehouse: Medallion Architecture](https://github.com/AnshulSilhare/sql-data-warehouse-project)
*Production-grade enterprise data warehouse on SQL Server.*
- **The Challenge:** Integrating siloed data from ERP and CRM systems into a single source of truth.
- **The Methodology:** Designed and built a 3-layer Medallion architecture (Bronze, Silver, Gold) on SQL Server. Engineered pure T-SQL ETL pipelines to serve dimensional models (Star Schema).
- **The Outcome:** Delivered business-ready fact and dimension tables, serving cleaned and validated data for analytics.
- **Metrics:** **3-Layer** Medallion | **Star Schema** | **Pure T-SQL** (no 3rd party tool).

#### 🏍️ [Rapido Logistics: Revenue Leakage & Loss Analytics](https://github.com/AnshulSilhare/Rapido_Logistics_Analytics_SQL)
*Analyzing logistics bookings to recover lost revenue.*
- **The Challenge:** Identifying revenue leakages and operational inefficiencies in shift schedules.
- **The Methodology:** Designed an operational MySQL database backend with 8 normalized entities (3NF). Analyzed 30,000+ bookings in SQL.
- **The Outcome:** Identified ₹251.9K in revenue leakage and flagged a 20% lead time spike in night shifts.
- **Metrics:** **30K+** Bookings Analyzed | **₹251.9K** Leakage Identified | **20%** Night Shift TAT Lag.

#### 🛍️ [Consumer Behavior Research: Personalization Paradox](https://github.com/AnshulSilhare/Consumer-Behavior-Research)
*Quantitative study using SPSS to analyze personalization algorithms vs. privacy concerns.*
- **The Methodology:** Formulated and analyzed survey responses (N=130) using Chi-Square and Spearman’s Rank tests in SPSS.
- **The Outcome:** Proven strong negative correlation (r = -0.47) between intrusiveness and data sharing, and positive correlation (r = 0.68) between trust and repurchasing.
